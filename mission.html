Mission

Bring an eagerness to build ideas with others to create thought-provoking solutions.

Vision

Personal Values [CROP]

Crops grow strong and bright with the help of a few crucial elements in balance.

In my case, those elements are Collaboration, Respect, Openness, and Punctuality.

Collaboration

One could agree this and communication are the most important part of any team environment, regardless of industry. Relying on others' strengths to cover my weaknesses and vice versa = ultimate results. Like yin & yang with more parts!

Respect

This value speaks to being organized and not only respecting others by making work comprehensive and easy to follow, but respecting myself by maintaining a clean workspace and appearance.

Openness

Being honest is crucial to me. When faced with a workplace decision, sharing ideas in a transparent, constructive manner is rewarding. I'd be nowhere without honest, constructive feedback.

Punctuality

It's frustrating when a friend says they'll be there in 20 and then show up an hour later. Being late puts a strain on relationships and productivity. For me, early is on time!
